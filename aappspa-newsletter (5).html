Business Review

Reassess Your Practice

As we begin, take a moment this month to reassess the current state of your practice.

Business Perspective

Review your service mix and determine whether your current balance of evaluations, treatment sessions, and group services aligns with both client needs and financial sustainability. Consider whether your pricing structure and payer contracts reflect current reimbursement trends and operational costs.

Operational Standpoint

Evaluate staffing patterns and workload distribution. Solo practitioners may consider opportunities to streamline administrative processes through automation, while larger practices may benefit from reviewing productivity expectations and team structure.

Policies & Procedures

Ensure that your HIPAA documentation, telepractice agreements, and intake processes are current. Confirm that documentation timelines are consistent with payer requirements and that your classification of employees and contractors aligns with current regulatory expectations.

AAPPSPA Interact

Respectful Communication & Conduct

AAPPSPA values respectful, inclusive, and professional dialogue. Members are expected to:

  • Engage respectfully in all organizational communications (in person, online, and via email)
  • Listen actively and consider differing perspectives
  • Avoid personal attacks or inflammatory remarks
  • Follow the communication expectations for the Listserv (see FAQ link)

Harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior toward other members or collaborators will not be tolerated.
